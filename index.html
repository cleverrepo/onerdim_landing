The Onerdim vibe

What we propose is not just an app, but simple rules that hold a community together.

Keep it real

  • Recommend a product or service you like
  • Explain why you are recommeding it
  • Don't share content for advertising purposes
  • Value real experiences

Honest reviews = happier humans

Be cool

  • Give constructive feedback
  • Do not insult or belittle
  • Respect privacy
  • Focus on the product, not the person
Not cool = Not here

  • Don't mislead people
